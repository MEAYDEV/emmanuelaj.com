About me

A little backstory.

I studied Mechatronics Engineering in Nigeria before moving to the US for my Master's in Computer Science at the University of Alabama at Birmingham. Since then, I've built things across software, AI, research, and a few apps people can use today.

More than anything, I just enjoy creating: hardware projects, software, AI experiments, making beats (mostly afrobeats), whatever turns an idea into something real. Lately my work has focused on computer vision, LLMs, and building products people actually enjoy using.

When I'm not building, I'm usually playing piano, on the soccer field, in the gym, hanging out, or listening to music.
